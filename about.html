About Us

We are a group of passionate people trying to redefine the durable medical equipment industry. We pride ourselves of being located in Chicago and you can find information about Graymont and Gray Medical here.

Graymont Medical

Graymont Equipment Distribution is a DME (durable medical equipment) provider that has been proudly servicing the Chicagoland area and beyond for the past 5 years. We have serviced professional athletes including Chicago Bears and Blackhawks players as well as patients from North Shore University, Midwest Orthopedics at Rush, OrthoIllinois and Illinois Bone & Joint Institute. From professional athletes to your grandparents to your young high-school student athletes, we strive to provide top notch customer service to you and your patients while they are rehabilitating from injuries and procedures.
