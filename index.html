What is Graymont Medical?

We are a medical distribution group dedicated to the physicians and patients we serve. Our mission is to provide best in class products and services that will optimize recovery and get people back to their active lifestyles as quickly as possible. Working closely with some of the most respected orthopedic surgeons in the world, our goal is to do our part in providing the proper equipment, instruction and service to enhance surgical outcomes.
